One More Time

According to me this phenomenon is not good at all. The growing popularity of English caused the fact that we overuse some words, for instance, weekend, big market, fast food, joystick or businessman. Maybe I have an obsession about it but I really do not like it. It is not that our native language is contaminated by foreign vocabulary only. Umberto Eco once said that such countries as the US are superpowers and that is why we must accept English as a lingua franca. Yes, indeed but the point is that we have to take into account that we live in Poland and first and foremost we are Poles.
